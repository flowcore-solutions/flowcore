## Brand Note

The current codebase, on-site copy, and official manufacturer site consistently use `Berlington Pumps`, while the requested positioning uses `Burlington Pumps`.

Use one approved master brand spelling across:

- Google Business Profile
- website copy
- local citations
- backlinks
- manufacturer listings

If `Burlington` is the target keyword but `Berlington` is the real manufacturer entity, keep the entity spelling consistent and treat `Burlington` only as a secondary search variation after confirmation. Mixed spelling across citations can dilute local rankings.

- 50% branded anchors
- 30% mixed branded + service anchors
- 20% exact-match anchors

Do not force exact-match anchors into every citation. Local listing platforms often rank better when the business name stays clean and the service keywords live inside the description.
